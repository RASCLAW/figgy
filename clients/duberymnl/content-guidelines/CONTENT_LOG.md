## APPROVED — High-Performing Posts (Historical)

These are from the 2020–2021 Facebook page. Patterns extracted for reference.

---

### ✅ POST: "Look good while protecting your eyes..."
**Format:** Simple benefit statement + COD/delivery hook
**Copy:** "Look good while protecting your eyes from too much sunlight and radiation with Dubery Polarized Sunglasses. Free shipping pa din and cod available nationwide. Limited stocks only! Order now!"
**Result:** 1,754 likes / 100 comments / 6 shares
**Why it worked:** Benefit-first → trust signal (COD) → scarcity/urgency. No fluff.
**Lesson:** Shortest, most direct posts outperform elaborate ones. COD + urgency = conversion.
**Status:** APPROVED (adapt: replace free shipping with Metro Manila same-day COD)

---

### ✅ POST: Classic Purple product shot
**Format:** Package/hero shot + specs + price
**Copy:** "Classic Purple Polarized lenses with Uv400 protection. Say goodbye to those squinty eyes! Price @ [price] Free shipping COD"
**Result:** 469 likes / 3 comments
**Why it worked:** Clean product photo + spec credibility (UV400) + relatable pain point (squinty eyes).
**Lesson:** UV400 + Polarized specs build trust. Squinting = relatable pain point that converts.
**Status:** APPROVED (adapt: ₱699 pricing, Metro Manila delivery)

---

### ✅ POST: Just "😎👌"
**Format:** Brand statement — minimal
**Copy:** Just two emojis
**Result:** 289 likes / 9 comments / 2 shares
**Why it worked:** Pure curiosity + confidence. Let the product photo do the work.
**Lesson:** Minimalist posts with strong product photography perform. Don't over-explain.
**Status:** APPROVED (use sparingly — max 1 per 10 posts)

---

### ✅ POST: "What's the magic word? FREE SHIPPING NATIONWIDE"
**Format:** Reveal/engagement hook
**Copy:** Suspense build-up → "The magic word is FREE SHIPPING NATIONWIDE!!"
**Result:** 226 likes / 8 comments / 1 share
**Why it worked:** Engagement bait → delivery trust signal.
**Lesson:** Reveal format works. Adapt hook for our model — reveal could be same-day delivery, bundle deal, or surprise promo.
**Status:** APPROVED (adapt: reveal = "Same day delivery, Metro Manila!" or bundle promo)

---

### ✅ POST: Outback product + colors available
**Format:** Product showcase + color variants
**Copy:** "Dubery Outback Polarized Series available in 4 different colors"
**Result:** 58 likes / 18 comments
**Why it worked:** Color variants drive comment engagement — people tag friends or ask which color.
**Lesson:** "Available in X colors" + product photo = comment magnet. Ask "which color is yours?" to boost further.
**Status:** APPROVED

**Why rejected:** Filipino audience skims in the feed. Long English text = ignored.

**Why rejected:** Retired. All content must use ₱699 / ₱1,200 bundle.

**Why rejected:** Business model changed. Metro Manila COD only. ₱100 delivery fee.

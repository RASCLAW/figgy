## Target Customer

**Primary:** Metro Manila buyer, 18–40, male or female.
- Commuter (jeepney, UV, car)
- Rider (motorcycle)
- Weekend outdoor person (drives to Tagaytay, Batangas, La Union, Rizal)
- Impulse buyer who doesn't want to wait 5–7 days on Shopee/Lazada
- Trusts COD (pays on delivery = zero risk)

**Pain points to hit:**
- Squinting in jeepney / EDSA glare
- Shopee/Lazada slow delivery
- Wanting to look good without spending a lot
- Not knowing polarized = better than regular tinted

## Voice & Tone

- **Language:** Taglish (mix of Tagalog + English). NOT pure English, NOT pure Filipino.
- **Energy:** Confident, cool, relatable — not corporate, not hard sell
- **Humor:** Light, self-aware Filipino humor (jeepney scenes, traffic, "pare" energy)
- **Copy length:** Short headlines (3–7 words max), 1–2 supporting lines max per callout

**DO:**
- "Chill lang, naka-Dubery ako."
- "Anti-squint sa jeepney glare!"
- "Pang-malakasang porma kahit siksikan."
- "Order now, hawak mo na bukas."
- "Bayad ka lang pag nasa kamay mo na."

**DON'T:**
- "Experience the premium quality of our polarized lenses..."
- Overly formal English
- Long paragraphs in the image
- Prices without the ₱ symbol

## Visual Standards

### Layout
- **Product is always prominent** — front and center or clearly visible
- **Price badge** always present on selling posts — bottom right, bold, circular or badge shape
- **DUBERY MNL logo** — top right or top left corner
- Bold headline: large, high contrast, readable at a glance in feed

### Colors
- Brand colors: Black, Red, White (from logo)
- Accent: Orange/gold (from lens reflection) — use for warmth and energy
- Backgrounds: Scene-appropriate (trail = green, beach = blue/sand, city = grey/urban)

### Typography
- Headlines: Bold, all-caps or title case
- Callouts: Smaller, inside speech bubbles or annotation arrows
- Always high contrast text vs background

### Product Photography
- Use real hero shots (white background) for product card posts
- Scene/lifestyle posts: product placed naturally in environment
- Never use low-res or unbranded product images

### Hashtags (standardized — always use this exact casing)
```
#DuberyMNL #PolarizedSunglasses #DuberyOptics #CODMetroManila #SameDayDelivery
```

## Content Formats

### 1. Comic / Local Humor
- Multi-panel or single-panel comic strip style
- Filipino characters in relatable settings (jeepney, traffic, outdoors)
- Speech bubbles with Taglish
- Best for: awareness, shares, new audience

### 2. Scene / Lifestyle Vibe
- Product in a real or AI-generated environment matching the vibe (trail, beach, city)
- Bold Taglish headline, brief supporting line, price badge
- Best for: aspirational reach, engagement

### 3. Brand Statement
- Minimal — product + background + 1 tagline
- "Pinoy Style, Pinoy Pride." energy
- Best for: brand recall, filler between selling posts

### 4. Infographic / Feature Breakdown
- Product photo with 3 callout bubbles pointing to features
- Taglish labels: benefit name (Filipino) + spec (English)
- Best for: education, first-time buyers, trust-building

### 5. Lifestyle with Models
- Real people wearing the product in Metro Manila or outdoor setting
- Versatility angle: "From weekdays to weekends"
- Best for: social proof, relatability

### 6. Package / Hero Shot
- Clean white background hero shot showing complete package
- Copy: model name + key spec + price
- Best for: direct selling posts, price reveal

## What NOT to Do

- Do not post without a price (unless it's a pure brand awareness post)
- Do not use ₱499 — that price is retired
- Do not say "free shipping" or "nationwide"
- Do not post blurry or AI-placeholder product images
- Do not use inconsistent hashtag casing
- Do not write long English body copy on the image
- Do not post without the DUBERY MNL logo visible
